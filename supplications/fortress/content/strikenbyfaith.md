<div class="duaextra" tabindex="0"> <div onclick = "void(0)"><strong>Transliteration</strong></div> <div class="extra">
133. `A'udhu billah

</div> </div> &nbsp;
<div class="duaextra" tabindex="0"> <div onclick = "void(0)"><strong>Translation</strong></div> <div class="extra">
